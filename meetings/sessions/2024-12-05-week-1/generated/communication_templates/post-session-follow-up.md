## Usage Guidelines

### Timing for Follow-Up Communications:
- **Immediate follow-up**: Within 24 hours of session
- **Feedback survey**: Send with immediate follow-up, leave open for 1 week
- **One-week check-in**: 7 days after session
- **Community engagement**: Weekly between sessions

### Personalization Tips:
- Reference specific moments or questions from the session
- Include names of active participants (with permission)
- Adapt language to match your team's communication style
- Add team-specific examples and use cases

### Metrics to Track:
- Email open and response rates
- Feedback survey completion
- Resource download/usage
- Community engagement (replies, questions, stories)
- Next session attendance compared to previous

### Continuous Improvement:
- Use feedback to adjust future session formats
- Track which resources are most popular
- Monitor community engagement levels
- Iterate on communication templates based on response patterns
