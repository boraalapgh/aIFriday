## Usage Instructions for Templates

### Customization Guidelines:
1. **Replace placeholders**: [TIME], [LINK], [DATE] with actual details
2. **Adjust tone**: Match your team's communication style
3. **Add team-specific context**: Reference relevant projects or challenges
4. **Include appropriate channels**: Tag relevant teams or individuals

### Timing Recommendations:
- **Initial announcement**: 1 week before session
- **Reminder message**: 2-3 days before
- **Day-of reminder**: 1-2 hours before
- **Follow-up**: Within 24 hours after session
- **Community building**: Weekly between sessions

### Engagement Tips:
- Use emojis to make messages visually engaging
- Ask questions to encourage participation
- Create polls for feedback and topic selection
- Share wins and success stories to build momentum
- Tag interested team members to increase visibility
