## Usage Instructions

### Adding New Ideas
1. Use `/add-topic-idea` command for quick entry
2. Or manually add to the appropriate section above
3. Include context: who suggested it, why it's valuable, rough timing

### Processing Ideas
- Topic manager will review weekly
- Ideas get moved to `pipeline/idea-backlog.md` for development
- Good fits go to `pipeline/medium-term.md` or `pipeline/priority-queue.md`
- Completed ideas get archived

### Idea Format
When adding ideas, include:
- **Topic**: Brief description
- **Context**: Why valuable, who requested
- **Potential Speaker**: If known
- **Timing**: Urgent/Soon/Future
- **Format**: Lightning talk/Deep dive/Clinic topic
